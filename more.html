Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Built Environment Indaba 2026.

What is the Built Environment Indaba?
The Built Environment Indaba is South Africa's premier gathering for infrastructure delivery, bringing together government, private sector, academia, and civil society to unlock construction capacity, funding, and transformation pathways. The 2026 edition focuses on practical solutions, pipeline access, and sector collaboration.
Who should attend?
Contractors (emerging and established), consultants, engineers, architects, government officials, financiers, suppliers, developers, policymakers, and anyone involved in infrastructure delivery or built environment projects in South Africa.
What are the registration fees?
Delegate registration: Free
Exhibition space: R12,000 to R258,234.46 EXCL VAT
Sponsorship packages: Diamond, Gold, Silver, and Partner tiers available. Contact us for detailed pricing.

Dates: 09‚Äì10 April 2026
Venue: The Canvas Riversands, Johannesburg
Format: Two full days of keynotes, sector sessions, networking lounges, and exhibition showcase.
What networking opportunities are available?
The Access & Growth Lounge runs throughout both days, offering curated connections with Access Bank, industry partners, Black Industrialist Program representatives, Built Environment Centre of Excellence, and key Construction Industry Partners. Evening cocktail sessions hosted by BBCBE Foundation provide additional informal networking.
How do I become a sponsor or partner?
We offer multiple partnership and sponsorship tiers (Diamond, Gold, Silver, and Strategic Partner). Each package includes exhibition space, branding, speaking opportunities, and delegate passes. Contact us at info@builtenvironmentindaba.africa or use the "Become a Partner" / "Sponsor Event" forms on our homepage.
